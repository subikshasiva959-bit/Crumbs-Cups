Sweet baked treat

Cookies are sweet baked treats enjoyed around the world. They come in many flavors and textures. From soft and chewy to crisp and crunchy.

Light, soft, and sweet with a smooth texture with icing and sprinkles.

Light, airy with a delicate sweet from whipped egg whites and sugar.

Buttery and nutty, rolled in powdered sugar with a sweet snowy finish.
